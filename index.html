WARWATCH

News from the source — not about the source

Live RSS feeds

News is a weapon. Especially during war. Most of us only see news from our own country — filtered by algorithm, shaped by whoever funds it. In troubled times, every country wants to protect its image.

We live in a moment where truth is diffused. We all seem to know something and nothing at the same time.

The internet is an empowering space — but only if we use it intentionally. Instead of letting algorithms decide what we see, we should have the option to look ourselves.

WarWatch shows you who funds each outlet. Because funding gives direction — on who does what, and for whom.

This is not a fact-checker. It is a window. What you do with the view is up to you.

Click any outlet name to see ownership and funding. Use the search bar to filter headlines across all columns by any keyword. Some feeds appear in their original language — right-click any headline and select "Translate to English" in your browser. We are working on bringing translations directly to the page.
